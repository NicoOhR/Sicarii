NoTeC

Nico OR
2024-29-9

To start, FSAE (Formula: Student Automotive Engineer) is a worldwide competition aimed primarily at giving university students a competition to exercise and apply the skills which they learn in the classroom. The competition is aimed predominantly at Mechanical Engineering, and in recent years with the advent of the EV competition, Electrical Engineering majors. By way of demand, however, the FSAE chapter at UT Dallas, a school overfilled with Computer Science Majors, several large software and firmware projects. Here I focus on the project which I have spent the most time on, the Data Acquisition Device (colloquially, NoTeC, a joke I will explain later) and will hopefully circle back to the other projects later.
